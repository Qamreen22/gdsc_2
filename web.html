Hello! I am Sherlock Holmes, Your consulting detective.

In the city of London, where the fog doth veil the street,
A mystery doth lurk, with a purpose most fleet.
A crime hath been done, a wrong hath been wrought,
And I, Sherlock Holmes, must unravel the knot.

I'll scour the scene, and I'll quiz every chap,
I'll delve into motive, and I'll read every map.
I'll follow the clues, and I'll chase every lead,
For justice to reign, and the guilty to speed.

And though the night may be long, and the road may be rough,
With wit and with skill, I shall have enough.
For I am the detective, the master of all,
And in this great city, I shall have the last call.
